HIVE Printed matter / 2021

A poster series was created comprised of three posters, representing how a group affects society and what leads them to the extreme. The internet is a highly accessible space to meet homogeneous people who have similar opinions. People in online groups share common values and opinions, which are reinforced within their selected groups. Group polarization appears through this process, which is a phenomenon that shows a pattern of group determination shifting to a more excessive side rather than individual decision-making. The text-based data that we researched and collected were used to develop graphics and typography that represent how groups are formed and agglomerated within online platforms. (co-work:Eunju Park)

____by_____ ______ Digital Publishing / 2020

An online reader was built with the fiction, 1984(by George Orwell). A sentence hit me; Less language Less think, and it gets concept for this online reader. You will see concise and shortened version of the original text in the left column. When you move cursor to the middle, then the longer text that includes adjective and adverb shows up. Lastly there is the entire text with adjective, adverb and modifier in the right column. Since Big Brother controls their citizen with less language so that they think less and become less smart, only you will see the text in the left column unless you wait for the black solid disappear and longer text shows up. Brackets mean some texts are censored and deleted. Go to the webiste

(im)mortal image Printed matter / 2020

It is known it has changed that in the past people document their life and memories with tangible materials but nowadays we just store them on computer. For my case, I upload memorable photos on Instagram Story. It becomes my virtual photo album and it comes as a question, how can I keep this in more precious way. Using Aceton to make images more hazel and feeling like faded in visualises memories lost in time. All selected images uploaded on Instagram Story are picked by my consideration so that it has opportunity to stay long physically.

Access to Tools Printed matter(Book) / 2020

Re-design of the “Access to Tools: Publications from the whole earth catalog” (by David Senior). Inspired by typical commercial catalog, all the titles and the body texts are separated. Title plays a role that catches reader’s eye with provocative words at the top of the page. All the titles are extracted from each paragraph and edited. Even though it is separated, there is number mark at before starting title and paragraph.

Can__Bring Down____? Printed matter(Book) / 2019

Redesign of the “Can Jokes Bring Down Governments?(by Metahaven). The book talks about online-based politic culture. Meme to criticise the government would spread with copy and paste by users. The text of redesigned is all copied with scanner. It visualises how information or texts will be shared on web. By copy and paste, the original would be distorted or be lose partially. For materialising, the book has some elements related with online features; pop-up window, pixel, the proportion of the web size.

Scribbling Video(1m) / 2019

It is from personal experience that I visited a hospital to see a friend who was going to get operation. It was disturbing when I found an 'arrow' mark on his leg. The mark was to distinguish which leg would get operation. Having inspired by this, the video was filmed to deliver how violent it is, and to trigger the feeling of being disturbing when you see the trace that people left ruthlessly on you.
