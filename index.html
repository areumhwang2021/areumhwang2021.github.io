Can__Bring Down____? Printed matter(Book) / 2019

Redesign of the â€œCan Jokes Bring Down Governments?(by Metahaven). The book talks about online-based politic culture. Meme to criticise the government would spread with copy and paste by users. The text of redesigned is all copied with scanner. It visualises how information or texts will be shared on web. By copy and paste, the original would be distorted or be lose partially. For materialising, the book has some elements related with online features; pop-up window, pixel, the proportion of the web size.

Scribbling Video(1m) / 2019

It is from personal experience that I visited a hospital to see a friend who was going to get operation. It was disturbing when I found an 'arrow' mark on his leg. The mark was to distinguish which leg would get operation. Having inspired by this, the video was filmed to deliver how violent it is, and to trigger the feeling of being disturbing when you see the trace that people left ruthlessly on you.
